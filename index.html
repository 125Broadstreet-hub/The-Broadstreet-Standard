Infusion Dosage Calculator

Butter • Oil • Milk • Cream • Syrup

What this tool does

This calculator helps you determine THC dosage for infused bases and recipes without spreadsheets or manual math.

  • Total THC in your infusion
  • Potency per tablespoon, cup, and milliliter
  • THC per serving in finished recipes

How it works

  • Enter cannabis weight and potency (THC% and/or THCA%)
  • Apply a realistic efficiency percentage (default: 75%)
  • Input how much base you made and how much you used
  • The calculator converts everything to a universal unit (mL)

Example

Input
7g flower • 21.5% THC • 75% efficiency
Infused into 1 cup butter
Recipe uses 0.5 cup • 10 servings

Output
~112 mg THC per cup
~7 mg THC per tablespoon
~5–6 mg THC per serving
